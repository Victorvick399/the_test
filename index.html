About Me

I’m an avid lover of technology and all things about it. Ever since I was young I have always been curious and very inquisitive about tech. That is what actually pushed me towards coding and I don’t think I have ever turned back since then. I was brand new to coding when I joined Moringa. The activities there were hands-on and team based. This improved me massively as it not only made me more responsible with deadlines but also it improved my leadership and interpersonal skills. There I have gained knowledge in Python, Javascript, PHP, HTML5 and CSS3. At Moringa I was also part of a team of six people who built a plugin for Equity bank. The technical hurdle we had to face was to learn a new language and how to use Magento, but we were able to achieve this and it is now available for other developers to download and install into their applications.
